Geek Bar Pulse 15000 Puffs: High-Capacity Disposable Vape with Smart Display and Dual Power Modes

The Geek Bar Pulse 15000 Puffs disposable vape combines massive e-liquid capacity with intelligent features for an extended, customizable vaping experience. Powered by a 650mAh battery and featuring the world’s first full-screen display, this device offers two distinct power modes—Regular Mode for up to 15,000 puffs and Pulse Mode for up to 7,500 puffs with intensified flavor and vapor. With a generous 16mL pre-filled capacity, integrated dual mesh coils, and draw-activation operation, it delivers consistent performance from first puff to last. Designed for wholesale and retail markets, the Geek Bar Pulse 15000 is available in multiple themed editions to suit diverse consumer preferences.

Product Features & Specifications

  • Dual Power Modes: Regular Mode (15,000 puffs max) for extended use, Pulse Mode (7,500 puffs max) for enhanced intensity
  • Revolutionary Display: World’s first full screen showing real-time battery level and e-liquid level
  • Massive E-Liquid Capacity: 16mL pre-filled reservoir with 5% (50mg) nicotine strength
  • Battery Capacity: 650mAh rechargeable battery with USB Type-C charging port
  • Advanced Coil Technology: Integrated dual mesh coil for rich, consistent flavor production
  • Simple Operation: Draw-activation system – no buttons, just inhale to vape
  • Multiple Editions Available: Thermal, Patriot, Savers, Mintz, Halloween, Sour, Frozen, Zodiac, Planet, and Zero Editions
  • Included Components: 1 Geek Bar Pulse 15000 Disposable device
